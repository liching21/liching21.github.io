Pronounciation Matters

A University Project

This Project is know as Pronouncaition Matters. It was a working prototype that was build in Wordpress during the final semester of Universitity. The goal of this project was to combine the client's blog, wiki and website into one simultaneously allowing the users to listen to the sample audio, record and replay their own voice recoring to assist them in improving their pronounciation.

This project has taken on a simplistic design, with the focus on functionality as oppose to appearance.
